About Us

Chum Buddies began in the humble city of Vancouver in 2003. Our goal is to help individuals overcome loneliness and make connections. We want to help people find other people when they are most needed. The four main benefits of Chum Buddies is to help you expand your network, practice your social skills, have more fun in your life and to improve your well being.

Work Culture

We believe in the power of our people. Working with Chum Buddies is fun and exciting. With our job rotation policy, there's never a dull moment in the office! Every Wednesday, we have our weekly company lunches.
